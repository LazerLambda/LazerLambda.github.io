## About Me

{{< figure class="avatar" src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/49939753?v=4" alt="avatar">}}

Currently, I am about to graduate from the Statistics and Data Science M.Sc. program from LMU Munich. Before the master's, I studied Computer Science and Statistics also at LMU. During my time at university, I also spent time at NTU Singapore and Tsinghua University in Beijing.

I am interested in the field of natural language processing, specifically in how models can understand the world based on the text modality. Unfortunately, even state-of-the-art models still lack proficiency in many common sense tasks, and I am interested in researching approaches to make these models smarter.
